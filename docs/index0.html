    Welcome to the Home of the Wizards of Lua

    The Wizards of Lua are a community of programmers and their friends who want to promote interactive programming into a new level of game experience.

    The Wizards of Lua is also a Minecraft modification that simply adds the /lua command to the game. Right this command you can enter sequence of statements that form a valid Lua program.

    For example, you could enter the following line into the chat:

    /lua for i=1,10 do place("stone"); move(UP); end

    This will create a pillar of stone directly in front of you, 10 meters tall.
